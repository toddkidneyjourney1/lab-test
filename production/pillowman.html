The Pillowman

Sponsored by TBA

Dates: October 8 - October 25

By Martin McDonagh

The Pillowman follows Katurian, a writer in a totalitarian state who is interrogated about the gruesome content of his short stories, which bear an uncanny resemblance to a series of child murders happening around town. A dark, gripping, and deeply thrilling examination of the power of storytelling.
