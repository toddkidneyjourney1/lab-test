The Pillowman

October 8 – 25, 2026

by Martin McDonagh

Step into the chilling, darkly comedic, and deeply unsettling world of The Pillowman. Martin McDonagh’s Olivier Award-winning play centers on Katurian, a fiction writer in a totalitarian state who is interrogated about the gruesome content of his stories—and their eerie resemblance to a series of actual murders. As the line between fantasy and reality dissolves, you’ll be gripped by a suspenseful, mind-bending tale that explores the power of storytelling, the scars of childhood, and the moral ambiguity of survival. It’s a bold, thought-provoking, and hauntingly brilliant production that will stay with you long after the final curtain falls!.
