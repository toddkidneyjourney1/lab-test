Fences

April 22 – May 9, 2027

by August Wilson

In collaboration with Theatre Conspiracy.August Wilson’s Pulitzer Prize-winning masterpiece, Fences, is a searing and powerful look at the life of Troy Maxson, a former star of the Negro Leagues who now works as a waste collector in 1950s Pittsburgh. As he struggles to provide for his family and reconcile his past dreams with his present reality, he builds both literal and figurative fences that threaten to divide those he loves most. It is a timeless, heartbreaking drama about legacy, sacrifice, and the enduring strength of the human spirit—an absolute must-see theatrical experience!
