Waiting for Godot

August 21 – September 6, 2026

by Samuel Beckett

mStep into the strange, hilarious, and haunting world of Waiting for Godot. Samuel Beckett’s masterpiece is the ultimate play about… well, waiting. Follow Vladimir and Estragon as they pass the time with witty banter, philosophical puzzles, and existential musings while they hope for the arrival of the mysterious Mr. Godot—who may never show up at all. It’s a brilliant, mind-bending journey that asks the big questions about life, purpose, and the human condition, all while keeping you laughing in the face of uncertainty.
