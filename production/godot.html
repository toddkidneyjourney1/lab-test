Waiting for Godot

by Samuel Beckett

On a barren road beside a single tree, two men wait for a mysterious figure who never arrives. A foundational masterpiece of 20th-century drama, Waiting for Godot explores meaning, existence, and the human condition in an uncertain world. Experience this profound, absurdist exploration of what it means to be alive and waiting.
