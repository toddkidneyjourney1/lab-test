God of Carnage

Sponsored by TBA

January 14 - Januart 31

By Yasmina Reza
Translated by Christopher Hampton

God of Carnage relates a comedy of manners without the manners. Two sets of parents meet up to deal with the unruly behavior of their sons. As the evening proceeds, the civilised veneer chips away and the parents become increasingly childish, resulting in a hilariously chaotic meltdown.
