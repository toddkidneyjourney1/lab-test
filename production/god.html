God of Carnage

January 14 – 31, 2027

by Yasmina Reza

Get ready for a hilarious and high-stakes collision of manners! God of Carnage is a razor-sharp, Tony Award-winning comedy about two sets of parents who meet to discuss a playground altercation between their sons. What starts as a civilized, polite conversation quickly devolves into a chaotic, no-holds-barred battle of wits, egos, and absurd hypocrisy. As their facades crumble, you'll be laughing out loud at the messy, relatable, and hilariously savage side of the human condition. It’s a fast-paced, edge-of-your-seat theatrical brawl you won't want to miss!
