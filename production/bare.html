Bare: A Pop Opera

February 18 – March 7, 2027

Music by Damon Intrabartlo, Lyrics by Jon Hartmere, Book by Jon Hartmere & Damon Intrabartlo

Experience the raw, electrifying energy of Bare, a powerful rock musical that peels back the layers of life at a private Catholic boarding school. As a group of students navigate the intense pressures of faith, identity, and sexuality, they struggle to find their authentic selves in a world that often demands conformity. It’s an unforgettable, heart-wrenching journey of discovery, longing, and the courage it takes to bare your soul. Don't miss this deeply moving and modern theatrical powerhouse!
