Bare a Pop Opera

Sponsored by TBA

February 18 - March 7

p>

Book by Jon Hartmere and Damon Intrabartolo
Music by Damon Intrabartolo
Lyrics by Jon Hartmere

Bare is an electrifying and emotionally raw rock musical that explores the lives of students at a private Catholic boarding school. As they navigate the complexities of identity, faith, love, and acceptance, the story reveals the hidden desires, painful secrets, and pressures of adolescence, ultimately culminating in a powerful, tragic, and resonant coming-of-age experience.
