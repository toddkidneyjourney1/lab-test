Bare: A Pop Opera

February 18 – March 7, 2027

Music by Damon Intrabartlo, Lyrics by Jon Hartmere, Book by Jon Hartmere & Damon Intrabartlo

Experience the raw, electrifying energy of Bare, a powerful rock musical that peels back the layers of life at a private Catholic boarding school. As a group of students navigate identity, faith, love, and acceptance, hidden desires and painful truths rise to the surface in this emotionally charged coming-of-age story.
