The Rivals

November 12 – 29, 2026

by Richard Brinsley Sheridan

In collaboration with Theatre Conspiracy.Get ready for a hilarious, high-stakes comedy of manners with Richard Brinsley Sheridan’s classic, The Rivals! Set in the fashionable, gossiping world of 18th-century Bath, this witty play follows the romantic entanglements of the wealthy and hopelessly romantic Lydia Languish, whose head is filled with novels. When her suitor, Captain Absolute, adopts a disguise to win her heart, chaos ensues as he must navigate the meddling of her formidable aunt, Mrs. Malaprop—whose legendary gift for mangling the English language is sure to keep you in stitches. It’s a delightful, fast-paced romp full of secret identities, jealous rivals, and comedic misunderstandings that celebrates the absurdity of love. Don't miss this timeless, laugh-out-loud theatrical treasure!
