The Rivals

Sponsored by TBA

Dates: November 12 - November 29

By Richard Brinsley Sheridan

The Rivals is a lively comedy of manners featuring romantic confusion, witty wordplay, and mistaken identities. Set in Bath, it follows Lydia Languish and her suitors through mischief, duels, and social satire, culminating in delightful theatrical chaos.
