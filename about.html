Our History

The Laboratory Theater of Florida is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit theater company dedicated to bringing intentionally innovative live theater to the Fort Myers community. Founded on the principle that theater should be a catalyst for conversation, we focus on cutting-edge works that challenge, inspire, and engage our audiences.

Our Mission
