Origin Story

The Laboratory Theater of Florida began in 2008 as a dream of founders Annette Trossbach, Nicole Rizley, and Louise Wigglesworth. We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit theater company dedicated to providing innovative, professional live theater to the Fort Myers community.

Our Mission

To provide a space for artists to take risks and for our community to experience the power of live performance in an intimate, thought-provoking setting.
