How to Find Us

The Laboratory Theater of Florida
1634 Woodford Ave, Fort Myers, FL 33901

Arrive 20–30 minutes early to park, check in, and settle in before curtain.

Parking

Parking is available in the lot adjacent to the theater and along nearby streets. Please observe all posted parking signs and private lot notices.

Tip: Save the address in your maps app before leaving home for faster arrival.
