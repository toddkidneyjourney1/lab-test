Board of Directors

Our theater is guided by a dedicated group of community leaders.

Waner Del Rosario

President

A professional with experience in arts, technology, and aviation, Waner brings creative vision and technical expertise. He is the founder of Dreniam Technology Solutions and a Technology Education Leader at Lee Health. He aims to increase access to the arts and foster a more inclusive artistic landscape.

Nykkie Rizley

Vice President

A founding member of The Laboratory Theater, Nykkie serves as the Director of Operations at Royal Shell Vacations and President of FAVR. She is an active community member and theater advocate.

Lauren Miller

Secretary

A high school educator in the Lee County School District, Lauren has been involved in theater since 2007. She is a dedicated volunteer at the Lab, having served as a director, stage manager, and actor.

Steven Coe

Treasurer

Active at the Lab since 2016, Steven is a local actor and artist with training in stage combat. He has been involved in over twenty area productions and is certified with Meisner International.

T. J. Albertson

Board Member

An active actor with the theater since 2017, TJ has performed in productions such as The Minutes and The Crucible. Outside of the theater, he works in sales.

Ryan Adair

Board Member

A three-time Emmy Award-winning television producer and assistant news director for local Southwest Florida affiliates. Ryan has been involved in theater since the age of 12.

W. Earl Sparrow, Jr.

Board Member

Affiliated with the Lab since 2021, Willie is a music director with a B.S. in Music from Florida A&M and a Masters in Music Education from the University of Florida.

Peter Ndiangui

Board Member

Peter Ndiangui serves as an Adjunct Professor at Florida Gulf Coast University.
