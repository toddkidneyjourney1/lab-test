🧪 What to Expect

  • Cold readings from the script — no memorization required
  • Movement exercises and creative improvisation
  • A warm, collaborative atmosphere (we're scientists, not judges)
  • Callbacks within 72 hours of your audition
  • All experience levels genuinely welcomed
  • Video submissions considered for remote candidates

🎬 Submit Your Audition Video

Upload to YouTube or Vimeo and share a public or unlisted link. 1–3 minutes is ideal.

🔬 Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a headshot?

Not required, but a recent photo in your video helps us put a face to the name.

Can I audition if I've never done theater?

Absolutely. The Lab was built on bold, first-time experiments. We love fresh energy.

What should I perform in my video?

A monologue, a scene, an original piece — anything that shows us who you are as a performer.

How long until I hear back?

We review all submissions and respond within 72 hours of the audition deadline.

Can I audition for multiple shows?

Yes! Select "Open call" in the form or submit separate forms for each production.

Is there a fee to audition?

Never. Auditions at The Lab are always free and open to the community.
